OpenDreamKit, whose name stands for "Open Digital Research Environment Toolkit for the Advancement of Mathematics", was a European project to create and strengthen virtual research environments in the field of mathematics and related fields.
It brought together software communities from mathematical software packages such as GAP, LinBox, LMFDB, MPIR, PARI/GP, SageMath, Singular, but also simulation tools from materials science such as OOMMF and general computational software such as Project Jupyter.
OpenDreamKit was funded from 2015 to 2019 by the ``Horizon 2020'' European Research Infrastructures Work Program (as project #676541).
OpenDreamKit was very active in developing and disseminating software tools and best practices. It organised or funded, or had members participate in several Software Carpentry workshops.
